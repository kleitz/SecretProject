* * * * * * HASHTAGS * * * * * * 
Put your specified hashtags in the hashtags.txt file.
Be sure to separate individual hashtags with newlines.
Don't add hashtag symbols (#) to the hashtags in hashtags.txt, this is done automatically.
You MAY NOT create blank lines in the hashtags.txt file.

* * * * * * SAYINGS * * * * * *
Put your what it tweets (what the bot says) in the sayings.txt file.
Be sure to separate individual tweets with newlines.
You MAY include hashtags in the tweets, but they will be randomly hashtaged already.
You MAY include user references (@) in your tweets.
You MAY NOT create blank lines in the sayings.txt file.
Try to keep your tweets at 140 characters, tweets that are not will be truncated.
